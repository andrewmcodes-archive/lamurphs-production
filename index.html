About

On Aug. 1, Laura Murph & family opened L.A. Murph’s Fine Cooking, a meals-to-go and catering business. Take a moment and browse this site and find out what we can do for you, and know that if you’re eating from/at L.A. Murph’s, it’s made from scratch with only the finest ingredients available. We appreciate your patronage!
